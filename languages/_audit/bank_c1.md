# CEFR C1 Vocabulary Bank — Audit

## Overview
Advanced (C1) layer of the multilingual vocabulary bank. C1 = register-marked, lower-frequency, abstract/academic vocabulary: nuanced verbs, formal abstract nouns, precise adjectives, and advanced connectors/adverbs. All entries are dictionary-attested in the seven target languages.

- File: `languages/_bank/c1.json`
- Count: **172 rows**
- Languages: en, de, fr, es, it, tr, ru
- Conventions: nouns carry citation article (de/fr/es/it); en/tr/ru bare. Verbs in the infinitive across all languages (en "to ..."). Adjectives, adverbs, connectors bare.
- Verified: parses as JSON; every row has 7 non-empty keys; English values unique and disjoint from `a1.json`; no leading/trailing whitespace.

## Composition
- Nuanced/formal verbs (~70): to abolish, to dispel, to undermine, to advocate, to mitigate, to exacerbate, to scrutinize, to refute, to substantiate, to curtail, to jeopardize, to relinquish, to thrive, etc.
- Abstract/academic nouns (~45): discrepancy, premise, connotation, paradigm, hypothesis, rationale, nuance, ambiguity, paradox, resilience, backlash, accountability, etc.
- Precise adjectives (~35): transient, exuberant, ambivalent, coherent, meticulous, plausible, feasible, pervasive, blatant, obsolete, lucrative, detrimental, etc.
- Advanced connectors/adverbs (~22): notwithstanding, ostensibly, arguably, albeit, hitherto, henceforth, conversely, inadvertently, inherently, implicitly, intermittently, whereas, etc.
