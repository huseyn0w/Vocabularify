# Product

## Register

product

## Users
People who want to grow their vocabulary in a foreign language passively, while doing
other work. The app sits as an always-on-top card or in the macOS menu bar and surfaces
one word + translation every few seconds. The user is rarely *in* the app — they glance
at it. So the glance has to be effortless to read and pleasant to keep around all day.

## Product Purpose
Make incidental, low-effort language learning feel calm and premium. Success is the user
leaving the window open for hours because it looks good on their desktop and never gets in
the way — learning by ambient exposure, not by drilling.

## Brand Personality
Quiet, precise, expensive. Three words: **luminous, composed, crafted.** It should feel
like a well-made instrument, not a toy or a gamified app. Confidence through restraint.

## Anti-references
- Gamified language apps (Duolingo-style): cartoon mascots, confetti, loud greens, badges.
- "Luxury = gold + glass": gradient-text, glassmorphism, drop-shadowed bevels, bling.
- Generic SaaS dashboards: tiny tracked-uppercase eyebrows on every block, identical card grids.
- Default Apple-system green/blue everywhere with no point of view.

## Design Principles
1. **The word is the hero.** Every surface exists to frame one luminous word. Chrome recedes.
2. **Restraint is the luxury.** One warm metallic accent (brass), deep neutrals, lots of air.
   Premium comes from typography, spacing and light — never from decoration.
3. **Motion is a focus-pull, not a parade.** The only choreographed moment is a new word
   resolving into focus. Everything else is fast, state-only feedback.
4. **Cohesion across windows.** Card, Settings, About and Import share one token system,
   one accent, one motion language. Nothing looks borrowed.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Body/label text meets WCAG AA (≥4.5:1); the large target word far exceeds it.
- Full `prefers-reduced-motion` path: the focus-pull degrades to a plain crossfade, no blur/move.
- Both a dark (Obsidian, default) and a light (Alabaster) theme, each independently legible.
- Keyboard navigation (Shift+←/→) and visible `:focus-visible` rings everywhere.
